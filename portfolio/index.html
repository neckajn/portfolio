How I Think

What fascinates me is the psychology behind why things work. Why do we trust some color schemes with our money? Why does one app feels like a warm hug and the others feel cold? Why do some interfaces makes us feel smart and some make us feel stupid? Understanding the 'why' of human behavior is what makes good design great design.

I believe the best solutions happen when analytical thinking meets intuitive understanding, when data informs aesthetics. Good design listens first, then responds with intention.
